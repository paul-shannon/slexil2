A Demonstration of SLEXIL

The first three lines of Dante's Inferno demonstrate slexil's coordinated presentation of audio (or video) with transcription, analysis and translation. The web page is created from an ELAN xml file or, more simply, from this YAML file.

We captured the Roberto Benigni's audio from here. Translation and morphemic analysis courtesy of the University of Alberta Department of Linguistics.

More elaborate presentations with open-ended annotation of transcriptions and morphemes are also possible.

For more information, contact Paul Shannon.
